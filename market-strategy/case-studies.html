(Phase 1 of 3 Q2 2019 – Q4 2020)

Customer Demographics Repository (CDR)

Reliable and targeted customer data is crucial to becoming an effective, data -driven enterprise. However, the company’s data existed in multiple databases and was hosted in silos that were clunky to use, if not entirely incompatible. There wasn’t a standardized format, which made analysis a complex and time-consuming process. Storage of historical data was limited and there wasn’t a place to store incoming customer data from new sales or R&D efforts. Overall, the system was rigid and difficult to scale for growth.

Situation

Reliable and targeted customer data is crucial to becoming an effective, data -driven enterprise. However, the company’s data existed in multiple databases and was hosted in silos that were clunky to use, if not entirely incompatible. There wasn’t a standardized format, which made analysis a complex and time-consuming process. Storage of historical data was limited and there wasn’t a place to store incoming customer data from new sales or R&D efforts. Overall, the system was rigid and difficult to scale for growth.

Solution

A new data warehouse, or CDR, was built on a modern, cloud-based platform with an architecture for storing high-volume, high-velocity and high-variety data. Already it has enabled access to diverse data sources and greatly improved analytic and statistical capabilities. Most impressive is the ability to conduct customer engagement analysis in areas like segmentation, profiling, and targeting, as well as predictive modeling for new products and services.

Driving Growth

Analytics help us understand what products would appeal to customers, predict who would buy them, and tailor messaging to customer preferences. With these data- driven insights, we can to introduce new products to market and diversify the services we provide.

Q3 2019 – January 2020

Business Intelligence Pilot Program for VNG Customer Profile

Situation

VNG was expanding the pipeline within the service territory and wanted to better understand the economic development outlook for the commercial, industrial, military and residential sectors.

Solution

We supported Business Intelligence with analytics that established market segmentation for the top 100 commercial customers and all 200 industrial customers. Also developed benchmarks and detailed reports on specific market segments.

Driving Growth

Analytics provided the information needed by Regulatory team to prepare rate cases and identify additional opportunities to pursue.

Q2 2020

COVID-19 Care Package Response

Situation

Many of our low income and elderly customers were facing hardships as a result of the pandemic. We wanted to support them and our communities, beyond natural gas distribution, by providing care packages filled with shelf-stable foods, fresh fruit and household essentials.

Solution

In partnership with Corporate Communications, Legal, Technology Organization, Customer Experience, and Training and Energy Efficiency, the effort was launched in less than two weeks. Shipt, the retail delivery company owned by Target, delivered the much-needed care packages to more than 5,000 customers across the GAS footprint.

Driving Growth

This was an innovative way for Southern Company Gas and its LDCs to differentiate ourselves as a utility a and create an uplifting experience in the communities we serve.

Q2 2020

AGL Recruitment Campaign

Situation

To meet AGL rate case requirements to hire new staff, Corporate Communications asked for our help to design a digital marketing campaign to boost HR recruitment efforts.

Solution

The campaign was deployed across Facebook/Instagram, Google, and LinkedIn which garnered more than 1.3 million impressions. From these impressions, more than 6K clicked on our ads. In less than a month, the campaign generated 1,700 candidates, and total applications were 20% higher compared to the same time period of the previous year. Naturally, the very best talent was hired.

Driving Growth

Rate cases are a major factor for generating growth and revenue.

December 2020 – ongoing

Illinois Customer Credit Analysis

Situation

Some customers delay or default on payment which affects revenue growth. In collaboration with the Credit team, we wanted to identify which customers fall into this group and create a strategy that would lead them to make on-time payments.

Solution

Data analysis and predictive algorithms are allowing us to gather valuable insights on customer payment history, behaviors, and trends. As more information is collected and a clear view of this demographic is established, we’ll finalize a strategy to reach these customers and suggest on-time payment opportunities.

Driving Growth

The data is the foundation for identifying customers who are delaying payment versus those who are unable to pay. It will enable us to develop a shut-off strategy designed to encourage customers to pay on time.

Q2 2020 – Q4 2020

Convenience Fee Research and Analysis

Situation

As part of a rate case strategy, it was imperative that we gained a better understanding of customer desire and payment behavior, as well as what our competitors are doing.

Solution

We partnered with Business Intelligence to establish research parameters that included a customer survey and a study of our competition. Also analyzed the payment trends of our low- income customers. The results have helped inform a more effective rate case strategy.

Driving Growth

The importance of rate case strategies should never be underestimated, as they directly impact revenue growth.
