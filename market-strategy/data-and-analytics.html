Opportunity Lies In The Data.

The market and regulatory environment are rapidly changing. Now is the time to leverage data and analytics as a strong foundation and business enabler for GAS.

Data and analytics are a corporate asset because they’re more deeply integrated into the ways we work than ever before. When unleashed and fully utilized, data and analytics will generate value across the business in areas like operational excellence, innovation, customer relationships, and risk management.

It’s All In The Customer Data Repository (CDR).

Market Strategy’s goal is to help transform the business as we transition from a noncompetitive landscape to a competitive one, where customers will have more choices selecting where they get their energy. Having customer data and the proper tools to analyze it is a critical asset to optimize strategic, tactical and operational business decisions. To get there, we’re partnering with TO, IT, SOCO and the Rates team and building a new data warehouse, known as the CDR, on a cloud-based platform with an architecture for storing high-volume, high-velocity and high-variety data.

First launched in the cloud in Q4 2020, the CDR boasts an ecosystem of capabilities that produce quality data assets, high data literacy, and value-added insights.

Included but not limited to

Capabilities

  • Data sharing
  • Anonymization of data for external analysis and use
  • Segmentation, profiling and targeting
  • Propensity modeling for new products and services
  • Large sample billing verification
  • Tariff compliance design
  • Rate design studies and modeling

The current and future uses of the new, cloud-based CDR are vast. It’s gives us the large amounts of data we need, and it’s a tool that lets us work with data itself.

Now, with the first analytics warehouse in the organization, we can examine large and varied data sets to uncover hidden patterns and correlations, market trends, and customer preferences. That data can then be used to inform strategies, monitor customer satisfaction, provide better service, cut costs, and more.

The CDR is the foundation to help us be more competitive and apply insights and intelligence to every decision, every opportunity, and every outcome.

The Customer Insights Book

This new tool provides demographic profiles of our customers and includes information like age, gender, geographical location, education level, marital status, and household income. In addition to these key characteristics, it also sheds light on behaviors and needs.

The content of the Insights Book will continue to grow as more and more data is analyzed. This critical information will allow departments all across GAS to more purposefully target and market to customers in an increasingly competitive landscape.
