About Us

Onward and upward

Our job, like yours, is to move the business forward. We do it by partnering with areas of the business that are ready for intense data analytics and a holistic view of the market. That combination, in addition to everyone’s expertise, will allow us to efficiently make improvements in everyday operations and future initiatives.

There's no time to lose

The market is challenging

  • Persistent flat growth
  • Limited organic growth
  • Marketplace competition

The financial landscape is active

  • Ongoing and increased external financial expectations
  • Internal net income and financial goals
  • Expansion in capital investments
  • Regulatory and policy sensitivities
  • Increased demand-side efficiencies

External pressures are increasing

  • Natural gas bans
  • Limitation on future growth
  • Regulations and increased scrutiny

Together, we can take on these challenges and reimagine the future of natural gas. We can utilize data to be predictive and develop smart strategies. We can meet our customers where they are now and will be in the future.

We can’t wait for opportunities to present themselves. We must create them.
