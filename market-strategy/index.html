Hello!

Southern Company’s Market Strategy group was created in February 2019 and is the brainchild of Southern Company’s chairman, president and chief executive officer Kim Greene and Nicor Gas’ president and chief executive officer John Hudson.

Our focus is to drive growth and market impact across the business. That means thinking strategically about the collaborating with you to find opportunities to better position our business for short and long-term growth.

Let’s meet our customers’ energy wants and needs by combining the best of our capabilities. Let’s think about new and alternative revenue streams. Let’s think about the big, big picture and get ahead of the curve.

The company’s success and growth demands that we partner with departments across the organization and embody future-focused thinking.
