Generator Product Launch in Georgia

Business growth is all about adding to our portfolio of products and services. With that in mind, we identified a customer need are satisfying that need with a Generac backup generator product launch. Backup generators run on natural gas and turn on automatically when an electrical outage occurs to restore power to a customer's home.

We know that:

  • Increased incidences of severe weather, that cause widespread power outages, are driving interest in generators. In fact, interest has never been higher.
  • The Covid-19 pandemic has kept millions of American workers and students at home and dependent on reliable power.
  • The global residential backup power market is expected to grow to $1.92 billion by 2024.1
  • 35% of Georgia’s residential customers said they were interested in backup generator and would prefer to get it from their utility provider.2

With a Q2 target launch date, Market Strategy is initiating a pilot program with AGL to offer Generac backup generators to residential customers. The program creates new avenues for meeting customer demand, increasing customer engagement opportunities, and positioning us as a go-to resource for energy solutions. Plus, the program creates a new revenue stream that will be given back to customers by putting downward pressure on rate increases.

1 Technavio, Residential Backup Power Market by Technology and Geography - Forecast and Analysis 2020-2024, published Jan 2020
