Community

We have been proud members of the communities we serve for more than 160 years, and we will continue to give back with our time, talent and resources.

Southern Company Gas and its subsidiaries actively participate in community initiatives and events that foster a healthy environment, promote education with a focus on STEM and provide warmth and comfort to those most in need.

Always here

When conditions worsen, our most important work begins. In times of extreme weather, Southern Company Gas employees have always been on the front lines making sure our customers continue to receive the natural gas they need to keep them warm.

How We Serve
