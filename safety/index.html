Safety

Our highest priority is, and always will be, ensuring the safety of our employees and everyone we come into contact with – including the people we work with and the people we serve.

Safety means a lot to us at Southern Company Gas. Our emphasis on the wellbeing of our people and customers starts with our Safety First operating standards. These standards help to ensure a safe and secure work environment across our operations and at our facilities.

Operating standards are just the beginning. We also recognize the importance of empowering our people to be leaders when it comes to safety. This means equipping each of our team members with the training, tools and resources they need to effectively respond to any situation.

To us, safety also means conducting around-the-clock monitoring of our pipelines to safeguard our infrastructure. In addition to making consistent upgrades and regular inspections, we use state-of-the-art technology to ensure the ongoing integrity of our systems.
