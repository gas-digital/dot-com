Charitable Giving

We want to be judged not only by the service we provide, but also for what we do to enrich the lives in the communities we serve.

We have been a catalyst for positive change in our communities for more than 160 years. That’s why we continue to give back with our time, talent and resources.

Virginia Natural Gas Wins Community Service Award

Virginia Natural Gas was the recipient of the Southern Gas Association’s 2018 Community Service Award for its Longleaf Pine Restoration Project. The annual award recognizes the most effective service or outreach program that improves the communities in which its natural gas industry members operate.

Southern Company Gas and its subsidiaries actively participate in community initiatives and events that foster a healthy environment, promote education with a focus on STEM and provide warmth and comfort to those most in need.

To make the most meaningful impact in our communities, we focus our support on these areas of giving:

Energy assistance

Energy conservation projects and emergency energy assistance programs that aid low-income senior citizens and households who need managing their natural gas energy consumption.

Community Enrichment

Programs that promote leadership by women and minorities; arts and culture; and business leadership and/or economic development.

Education

Public secondary schools (K-12); public and private colleges; and universities, with an emphasis in literacy, math and science.

Environmental stewardship

Projects that promote clean air, conservation and green space.

Nicor Gas Named Environmental Champion

In 2018, Nicor Gas was named a Top Utility Environmental Champion, according to a new Cogent Reports survey of utility customers nationwide conducted by Market Strategies International. The results designate the top 43 utilities throughout the country that have shown dedication to environmental stewardship and are published in a report titled Utility Trusted Brand & Customer Engagement™ Residential.
