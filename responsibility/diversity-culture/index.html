Diversity & Culture

At Southern Company Gas, we believe our diversity cultivates new perspectives and innovation that enhances our ability to deliver superior results for our customers, employees and shareholders.

We’re committed to promoting an inclusive workforce that reflects the diversity of the customers we serve and a supportive environment so our people are able to achieve their personal and professional goals.

We promote inclusiveness – both in our workforce and our ways of thinking – so that all employees feel valued and respected for their contributions to the business.

We strive to provide an environment where our employees are enriched by being here and are able to do and be their very best. We do this by creating an inclusive culture with a workforce that reflects the diversity of the communities we serve.

Diversity at Southern Company Gas

We define diversity not just as race or gender but also as the differences in the backgrounds, experiences, perspectives and ideas of our employees.

We believe diversity is an asset that brings value to our organization. Viewing diversity as an asset means that each individual is valued for who they are and what they bring to our business. Life experiences and personal perspectives enable us to respond and think differently, approach challenges and solve problems differently, make suggestions and decisions differently, and see different opportunities and risks. The more assets an organization possesses, the greater its ability to innovate, grow and compete.

Inclusion at Southern Company Gas

We define inclusion as a work environment without barriers where all employees feel welcomed, valued, respected and engaged. In this way, we recognize, respect and value each individual. Inclusion leverages the power of diversity to achieve our business strategies and goals.

Our strategy

By providing an inclusive work environment the company utilizes the diversity of our workforce to drive results, performance, engagement and innovation.

Our diversity and inclusion strategy includes:

  • Creating an environment where employees have the opportunity to reach their full potential.
  • Developing and sustaining relationships with diverse suppliers to broaden access to products and services and provide economic inclusion.
  • Forming partnerships to ensure growth and vitality of the diverse communities in which we live, work, serve and create business alliances.
Our Business

Our four natural gas utilities that deliver clean, safe, reliable and affordable natural gas to approximately 4.2 million customers.
Responsibility

We have been a catalyst for positive change in our communities for more than 160 years. That’s why we continue to give back with our time, talent and resources.
Investors

We’re committed to supporting and improving our communities and environment at the local level and beyond, while conducting business with honesty, integrity and fairness.
