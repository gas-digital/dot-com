Safe energy

America’s natural gas companies invest $86.6 million each day to ensure the safety of natural gas transmission and distribution systems for our customers and communities.

“Safety is and always will be the number one priority for the natural gas industry. Any natural gas incident, no matter the size, is one incident too many.”

Southern Company Gas is constantly innovating to protect our employees and those we serve. Most recently, when the pandemic hit, we leveraged predictive analytics to determine at-risk sites near hospitals, nursing homes and other critical facilities and deployed additional resources to ensure safe continuity of service.

Safety first

Operating standards are just the beginning

We also recognize the importance of empowering our people to be leaders when it comes to safety. This means equipping each of our team members with the training, tools and resources they need to effectively respond to any situation.

Safety means a lot to us

It means conducting around-the-clock monitoring of our pipelines to safeguard our infrastructure.

State-of-the-art technology

In addition to making consistent upgrades and maintaining the safe condition of our pipelines through regular inspections, we use state-of-the-art technology to ensure the ongoing integrity of our systems.
