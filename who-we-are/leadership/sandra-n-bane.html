Sandra N. Bane

Retired Audit Partner, KPMG, LLP

Sandra N. Bane was named Audit Partner of KPMG LLP in 1985 where she served until her retirement in 1998. She was responsible for heading the Western Regions Merchandising practice and establishing the Employee Benefits audit specialist program in Southern California.

Ms. Bane also served as partner-in-charge of the Western Regions Human Resource department for two years and as an accountant with increasing responsibilities during her 23-year tenure with the company.

Ms. Bane currently is a Director of Big 5 Sporting Goods Corporation and Transamerica Asset Management Group, a mutual fund company. She was a director of PETCO Animal Supplies, Inc. from 2004 until 2006. Ms. Bane has also served as a member of the board of several nonprofit institutions in her community.

Ms. Bane has been a Director of Southern Company Gas (formerly AGL Resources) since February 2008.
